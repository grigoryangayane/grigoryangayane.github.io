Naval Sea Systems Command (NAVSEA) Ship Maintenance Performance Group (SMPG) courses

I work with Andrew Collins, Jim Leathrum, T. Steven Cotter, Ross Gore, and Christopher Lynch. Courses are week-long, graduate-level boot camps that introduce the theory and practical exercises to manage and analyze data effectively. In 2022 NAVSEA SMPG course was selected as 2022 Department of the Navy Information Technology Award Recipient.

"These boot camps bring technical, data analytics knowledge directly to ship maintenance domain experts, enabling the effective analysis of data by those closest to the problems, and builds skillsets within the organic workforce that can be used to improve the quality of decisions needed to correct these problems." ~ The Department of the Navy's Information Technology Magazine

  • Data analytics course - 20 students
    • Introduction to Statistics, Statistics with R, SQL with R, Machine Leaning
  • Predictive analytics - 20 students
    • Time series analysis, Forecasting, Generalized Linear Models, Classification and Deepr Leaning
  • Data modeling - 20 students
    • Stepwise Regression, Best-subsets regression, Deep Learning models
  • Data management - 20 students
    • Data wrangling, Data cleaning, Date and categorical data
